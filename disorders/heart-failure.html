Overview

Complex clinical syndrome resulting from structural or functional cardiac abnormalities that impair ventricular filling or ejection. This comprehensive resource provides evidence-based information on clinical presentation, diagnosis, treatment, and management of Heart Failure.

Key Facts

  • • 86,000 deaths annually annual impact
