Pericarditis

Inflammation of pericardium characterized by chest pain and pericardial friction rub. Can be acute, recurrent, or chronic.

Clinical Guidelines

Management of Pericarditis should follow current evidence-based guidelines from major cardiovascular societies. Treatment should be individualized based on patient characteristics, disease severity, and comorbidities. Regular follow-up and monitoring are essential for optimal outcomes.
